🔒 Privacy Policy

Konstruksyon Estimator PH · ph.konstruksyon.estimator

📅 Last Updated: March 1, 2026  ·  Effective Date: March 1, 2026
Summary: Konstruksyon Estimator PH does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device. No account required. No tracking.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Konstruksyon Estimator PH ("the App"), a construction material estimator tool designed for Filipino builders and homeowners. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent. The App does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to our servers
  • Any financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device data

3. How the App Works

100% Offline & On-Device: All calculations are performed locally on your device. Your input data (room dimensions, material prices, etc.) never leaves your phone.

The App stores your Pro license status and saved estimates locally on your device using localStorage. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Only accessible by the App itself

4. In-App Purchases (Pro Version)

The App offers an optional Pro upgrade through Google Play Billing. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is handled entirely by Google Play
  • We do not receive or store your payment details
  • Google's own Privacy Policy applies to payment processing
  • We only receive a confirmation token that verifies your purchase

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle payment data.

5. Internet Permission

The App requests internet permission solely for:

  • Verifying Pro license purchases through Google Play Billing
  • No data about you or your calculations is transmitted

The App works fully offline for all calculation features.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, social media SDKs, or tracking services. There are no third-party services that collect data through this App.

7. Advertisements

The free version of the App may display placeholder advertisement slots. These are currently not connected to any ad network and do not collect any data. If advertisements are enabled in future versions, this policy will be updated accordingly.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is a professional construction estimator tool intended for general audiences (ages 3+). It does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no data to secure on our end. All locally stored data (Pro status, saved estimates) is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms.

10. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete from our servers. To remove all locally stored app data, simply uninstall the App from your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Compliance

This App complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Republic Act 10173 – Data Privacy Act of the Philippines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
